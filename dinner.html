Vegetarian Wrap

Lay the tortilla flat on a clean surface.Arrange the sliced grilled chicken evenly down the center of the tortilla.Add the mixed bell peppers, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion on top of the chicken.Season the veggies with salt and pepper to taste.Place a handful of fresh greens on top of the veggies.Optional: Add crumbled feta cheese, sliced olives, or avocado slices for extra flavor.Starting from one end, tightly roll the tortilla to create the wrap.Cut the wrap in half at a slight diagonal for easier handling.Serve immediately or wrap it in parchment paper or foil for a portable lunch.
