Avocado Egg Toast

Avocado egg toast is a trendy, nutritious breakfast. Slice an avocado onto whole-grain toast, then top with a perfectly poached or fried egg. The creamy avocado complements the runny yolk, creating a delicious and satisfying combination. Vegan Cauliflower Gyros

In a large bowl, mix together olive oil, ground cumin, ground coriander, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper.Add the cauliflower florets to the spice mixture and toss until evenly coated.Spread the cauliflower on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Roast in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until the cauliflower is golden brown and crispy on the edges.Warm the pita bread or flatbreads.Spread a generous spoonful of Tzatziki sauce on each bread.Add a portion of the roasted cauliflower on top of the Tzatziki.Top with sliced tomatoes, sliced red onions, and shredded lettuce.Optionally, drizzle with additional Tzatziki sauce.Serve immediately and enjoy your Vegan Cauliflower ..
